==== GAMEPLAY ====
The game is an adventure game similar to Myst (or at least more similar to Myst than other adventure games I've played). You have no visual inventory, no on-screen character representation, and you never see any people. Yes, I know Myst has people in it, but when it does it isn't very often. Click on objects and navigate the room by clicking the far left and far right of the screen. (The cursor will change to let you know when you will change views). Check out the walkthrough below if you get stuck. Enjoy! :-)

==== ABOUT ====
I recently got into the Myst games (and lore) and finished Myst in about two days. I'm now working on reading the Book of Atrus before I start playing/experiencing Riven. After playing Myst I decided I would make an adventure game for the upcoming Ludum Dare (because it was only a week or so away at the time). I had a little mishap around the 45 hour mark, where I accidentally clicked a shortcut I have defined in Vim that exectutes '%!xxd -r' and then saves and quits. This totally screwed my game.py file over and I had to revert to the backup I made the night before. Luckily I have my code pretty well spread out across multiple files, so all I had to do was type out a bunch of repetitive and way-too-drawn-out code to get back up to speed. The moral of the story: don't put off putting your project on GitHub because you cannot think of a good repo name. Also, don't put volatile Vim shortcuts where you might accidentally press them.

Walkthrough

Move your mouse next to the hints to reveal as much or as little as you'd like.

Part 1

Hint 1: You need to find the key to the closet

Hint 2: Try reading the diary one more time

Hint 3: The key to getting the key is connecting the diary entry with the 10 medals on the wall

Hint 4: Get rid of the medals that aren't well-deserved

Hint 5: Take only the 2nd, 3rd, and 8th medals off the wall

Hint 6: The key is near the medals

Hint 7: Knock over the picture and pick up the key

Part 2

Hint 1: Read the article in the drawer a bit more carefully

Hint 2: Read the title of the article and then visit the closet again

Hint 3: Remove only the blue shirts from the closet

Hint 4: Pick up the gun

Hint 5: It is now safe to leave the house
