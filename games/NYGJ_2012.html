Controls

Hold UP and DOWN to move up and down
Hold SPACE to move right two player widths
You can get hit 3 times before the game ends
Try to get a high score!

About

This game was developed on a Raspberry Pi computer using vim.tiny, except for the finishing touches and final playtest.

I didn't get to spend as much time on this as I would have liked (due to homework, problems with my Raspberry Pi, and problems with getting Git to work) although I did get done most of what I wanted to get done.
